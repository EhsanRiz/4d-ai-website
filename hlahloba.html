The Challenge

Vehicle Claims Processing is Broken

Insurance companies across Southern Africa face mounting challenges with traditional claims assessment methods.

Slow Processing

Manual assessments take 3-7 days on average, frustrating policyholders and delaying settlements.

Inconsistent Results

Different assessors produce different estimates for the same damage, leading to disputes and inefficiency.

Expensive Experts

Skilled assessors are costly and in short supply, especially for high-volume, straightforward claims.

Fraud Vulnerability

Manual review misses sophisticated fraud patterns - recycled photos, staged damage, inflated estimates.
